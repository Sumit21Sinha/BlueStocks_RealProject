# Day 4: Performance & Risk Analytics Report

## Objective

The objective of Day 4 was to evaluate mutual fund performance using return, risk, and ranking metrics.

---

## Metrics Calculated

### 1. Daily Returns

Calculated daily percentage change in NAV for each mutual fund.

### 2. CAGR

Computed annualized growth rate for every scheme.

### 3. Volatility

Measured return fluctuations using standard deviation of daily returns.

### 4. Sharpe Ratio

Evaluated risk-adjusted performance using a risk-free rate of 6%.

### 5. Maximum Drawdown

Measured the largest peak-to-trough decline experienced by each fund.

### 6. Fund Ranking Score

Created a composite score using CAGR, Sharpe Ratio, and Maximum Drawdown.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund ranked first overall.

The fund achieved the highest composite score among all schemes.

### 2. Flexicap and Large Cap categories performed strongly.

Several top-ranked funds belonged to diversified equity categories.

### 3. High CAGR funds generally achieved superior rankings.

Long-term growth contributed significantly to overall score.

### 4. Drawdown played a major role in rankings.

Funds with lower drawdowns received better scores.

### 5. Sharpe Ratio differentiated risk-adjusted performers.

Funds with strong returns but excessive volatility ranked lower.
