# Day 5 – Power BI Dashboard Development

## Objective

Build an interactive Mutual Fund Analytics Dashboard using Power BI.

---

## Datasets Used

1. fund_analytics_final.csv
2. clean_transactions.csv
3. 03_aum_by_fund_house.csv
4. 04_monthly_sip_inflows.csv
5. 06_industry_folio_count.csv

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Executive Overview

KPIs:
- Total Funds
- Average CAGR
- Average Sharpe Ratio
- Average Fund Score

Visuals:
- Top Ranked Funds
- Top CAGR Funds

---

### Page 2 – Fund Performance

Visuals:
- Top Sharpe Ratio Funds
- Most Volatile Funds
- Lowest Drawdown Funds
- Risk vs Return Analysis

---

### Page 3 – Investor Insights

Visuals:
- Age Distribution
- Gender Distribution
- T30 vs B30 Investors
- State-wise Investments
- Payment Mode Distribution
- Income Distribution

---

### Page 4 – Industry Trends

Visuals:
- SIP Inflow Trend
- Active SIP Accounts Growth
- Industry Folio Growth
- Top Fund Houses by AUM
- Number of Schemes by Fund House

---

## Key Insights

1. Equity mutual funds delivered the highest CAGR.
2. Higher CAGR funds generally exhibit higher volatility.
3. T30 cities contribute the majority of investments.
4. SIP inflows have shown strong growth over the last three years.
5. Industry folio counts continue to increase steadily.
6. ICICI Prudential and SBI Mutual Fund manage among the highest AUMs.

---

## Outcome

Successfully developed a multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard for mutual fund performance, investor behavior, and industry trend analysis.
